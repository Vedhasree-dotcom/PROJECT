Our Iconic Dish

Tamarind Curry (Pulinji) : Kerala special tasty tamarind curry (pulinji)

Mrs. Saraswathi Amma is the founder of Saraswathi Pickles, a business dedicated to preserving the rich flavors of traditional South Indian pickles and unique pulinji. Established in 2023, the journey began with a simple idea which is this special Tamarind curry (pulinji). This nostalgic flavor inspired the creation of Saraswathi Pickles. Kerala style Pulinji (also called Pulinkari in some regions) is a traditional sweet-and-sour ginger-tamarind preparation, very popular in Sadya (the grand vegetarian feast served on banana leaf). It’s one of those dishes that balances the heavy meal with its tangy, spicy, and slightly sweet flavor. It is served in small portions on the side, not as a main curry. It preserves for a few days because tamarind and jaggery act like natural preservatives. The word pulinji came from " Puliyum Inji " = Tamarind (Puli) + Ginger (Inji).

“Our grandma’s Pulinji — not quite a pickle, not quite a chutney ; but the heart of every Kerala Sadya. A sweet, sour, and spicy tradition that makes every meal unforgettable.”
