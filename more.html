Story Behind Saraswathi Pickles

Long before “pickles in bottles” became common, our grandmother would carefully prepare them in clay jars, letting the flavors grow richer with time. The aroma of spices, the tang of tamarind, and the warmth of ginger filled our home, making every meal special. Among all her creations, one stood out — Pulinji, a kerala pickle like dish - the sweet and tangy ginger pickle. It wasn’t just food; it was her signature, a taste that carried love, care, and tradition in every spoonful. Her pickles weren’t just condiments, they were memories bottled with love. And among them all, her Pulinji the sweet, spicy, and tangy ginger pickle became legendary in our family and neighborhood. A spoonful of it could brighten even the simplest meal, and every jar she made was a piece of her heart shared with others. Today, through Saraswathi Pickles, we continue her legacy. Every jar is made the way she taught us patiently, traditionally, and with the same heart that made her pickles unforgettable. When you taste these Pickles, you’re not just enjoying a recipe you’re tasting a story passed down through generations.

Our Mission

Saraswathi Pickles, we celebrate India’s culinary heritage through authentic pickles and pulinji. Committed to quality, we use 100% natural ingredients and honor traditional recipes. By sourcing locally and embracing flavor innovation, we connect people through the vibrant, diverse tastes of Indian cuisine.

Our Vision

Saraswathi Pickles, we envision a world where the rich flavors of Indian cuisine are celebrated in every home. We aspire to be a trusted name for authentic pickles and pulinji's, connecting people through the joy of cooking and the warmth of shared meals, fostering cultural appreciation.
