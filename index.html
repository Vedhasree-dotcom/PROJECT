About Us

Saraswathi Pickles: A Culinary Journey Through Nostalgic Flavors

Mrs. Saraswathi Amma is the founder of Saraswathi Pickles, a business dedicated to preserving the rich flavors of traditional South Indian pickles. Established in 2023, the journey began with a simple idea during a meal shared with a guest. The appreciation for the unique, nostalgic flavors inspired the creation of Saraswathi Pickles. This name being to Amma's name because none other name suits to her own identity. This brand aims to bring back the essence of South Indian culinary traditions. The brand caters to both offline and online customers, ensuring access to healthy, hygienic, and high-quality vegetarian pickles. With a commitment to authenticity, Saraswathi Pickles has built a reputation for delivering the genuine taste of traditional pickles while maintaining modern standards of quality and hygiene.
